Novel record

The People of the Mist

8 editions 6 illustrators 41 illustrations

In the African romance The People of the Mist British explorer Leonard Outram seeks out a mysterious lost civilization and their treasure of precious gems.

Tit-Bits Weekly serialized PM from December 1893 to August 1894. The first book edition, published by Longmans, Green, and Co., London, on 15 October 1894, featured 16 illustrations by Arthur Layard. Longmans issued 10,000 copies in this printing. Longmans, New York, included the Layard illustrations in the first US edition published in 1894. George Newnes, London, published an edition c. 1906, which features 8 illustrations by Cyrus Cuneo. In c. 1923 Hodder & Stoughton, London, published an edition with an illustrated dust jacket. Macdonald & Co., London, published an edition of PM illustrated by Jack Matthew in 1951, 1st thus.
