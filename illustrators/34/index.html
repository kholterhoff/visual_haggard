Illustrator record

Walter Paget

Illustrator and painter Walter Stanley Paget (1863-1935), also known as Wal Paget, attended the Royal Academy Schools where he won the gold medal. Walter was the youngest of three artistic brothers, Henry Marriott Paget RBA (1856-1936) and Sidney Edward Paget (1860-1908).
